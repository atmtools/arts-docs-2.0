Agenda emission_agenda

Thermal emission source term.

This agenda shall return the emission at one position along
the propagation path. The source term equals the Planck function as
long as thermodynamic equilibrium (LTE) can be assumed, while for
non-LTE conditions much more complex calculations are required.
