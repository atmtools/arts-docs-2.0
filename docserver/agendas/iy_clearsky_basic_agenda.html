Agenda iy_clearsky_basic_agenda

As iy_clearsky_agenda, but lacks all support for jacobian and
auxiliary variables.

This agenda is used by scattering methods without support for the
jacobian and auxilary parts, in order to calculate unscattered
radiation for the speciefied position and line-of-sight. As this
agenda has much fewer input variables, this simplifies the interface
and maintaince of those scattering methods.

Group: Agenda

Variables

OUTiy(Matrix) Monochromatic pencil beam radiance spectrum.
INrte_pos(Vector) A geographical position for radiative transfer calculations.
INrte_los(Vector) A line-of-sight for radiative transfer calculations.
INcloudbox_on(Index) Flag to activate the cloud box.
